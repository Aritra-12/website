Gaussian Curvature

The Gaussian Curvature is a wonderful initiative, created by a group of highschool students passionate about mathematics and dedicated teachers. It is an international organization, which provides free handouts and holds several competitions.

I am a member of the USAMO/IMO Gaussian Curvature team, and along with my hard–working colleagues we proudly organize various yearly international competitions, among which are:

  • GIMO – Gaussian International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO type contest)
  • GAMO – Gaussian Mathematical Olympiad (USAMO type contest)
  • GJMO – Gaussian Junior Mathematical Olympiad (USAJMO type contest)

Gaussian Curvature also offers several handouts, dedicated to the olympiad preparation. There are various topics, including the LTE lemma, the PQR method in inequalities, advanced geometry lemmas and more.

In order to browse the rest of the GC handouts, browse the list of competitions, their rules, problems and prizes, visit the official Gaussian Curvature website and look out or the AoPS posts in the AoPS Mock Contests and Peer to Peer Programs forums.
